About Car Parking System

Learn about our mission and technology

Our Mission

To provide an efficient and user-friendly parking management system that leverages the power of data structures and algorithms.

The Car Parking System is designed to optimize parking space utilization, reduce waiting times, and provide a seamless experience for both parking administrators and vehicle owners.

Key Features

  • Circular Linked List

    Efficient management of parking slots in a circular manner

  • Queue System

    Fair waitlist management for vehicles when parking is full

  • Hash Table

    O(1) time complexity for vehicle lookups by license plate

  • Optimized Performance

    Using DSA principles for high efficiency

Data Structures Used

Circular Linked List

A circular linked list is used to represent parking slots. This allows for efficient traversal and management of slots in a circular fashion, making it easy to find available slots.
